    About Us

    Pioneering AI & Data Science Education

    The Department of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science at Sapthagiri NPS University delivers world-class education at the intersection of technology, innovation, and real-world problem solving.

    Our curriculum equips students with cutting-edge skills in machine learning, deep learning, data analytics and AI engineering — preparing them for tomorrow's digital economy.

    With state-of-the-art labs, industry-aligned projects, and expert faculty, we create an environment where curiosity meets capability.
